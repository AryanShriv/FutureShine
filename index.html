About the Event

Future Shine Industrial Award 2025 celebrates innovation, entrepreneurship, and collaboration across India. Join us in honoring the outstanding contributions of startups, SMEs, and industry leaders.

Encouraging Innovation:

Promoting new startups and small businesses while fostering innovation capacity across industries

Honoring SMEs & Startups:

Recognizing exceptional contributions of Micro, Small & Medium Enterprises and Startups to India's economy

CSR & Innovation Bridge:

Connecting corporate CSR activities with innovation and entrepreneurship for sustainable development

Vocal for Local:

Supporting MSMEs in strengthening local products and services for national and international recognition

Expert Jury Panel:

CEOs and Directors from India's top 10 companies alongside industry experts ensure fair evaluation

Strategic Partnerships:

Building collaboration between large corporates and SMEs through supply chain, technology sharing and networking
